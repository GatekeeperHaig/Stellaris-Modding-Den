### The Hurrians were an ancient peoples in the Bronze Age Near East, mainly residing in northern Mesopotamia. The most powerful Hurrian state were the Mittanni, who are thought to be of Indo-European origin, so a number of upper-class Mittanni names and Mittanni deities are likely of non-Hurrian origin. However, they have been included in my random generation of fictional Hurrian words and names for greater variety
