### Steps to making these words:
###	1. Google translate the opening and ending paragraphs of The Catcher in the Rye into Korean.
###	2. Throw in romanization of said Korean text into the Cthulhu-themed language mixer here: http://chaoticshiny.com/langreplace.php
###	3. Throw in the words it spews out into a markov generator, and voila!
